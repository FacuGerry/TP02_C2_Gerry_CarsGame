# TP02_C2_Gerry_CarsGame

Itch: https://facugerry.itch.io/car-go-vroom

## Car go vroom? CAR GO VROOM!!!
[EN] Welcome to the tracks! Here, you'll have to be the best driver ever. Drive laps around the circuits while you shoot the enemies that want to kill you. You can play endless mode to practice your laps. Choose from 4 different cars and 3 different tracks to drive around!!

[ES] Bienvenido a la pista! Aquí, tendrás que ser el mejor corredor del mundo. Maneja a través de circuitos mientras que disparas a enemigos que quieren matarte. Puedes jugar al modo endless para practicar tus vueltas. Elige uno de los 4 autos y las 3 pistas para empezar a correr!!

> [!TIP]
> ### Controls:
> | KEY     | FUNCTIONALITY |
> | ---      | ---       |
> | W  | Accelerate  |
> | A  | Turn Left |
> | D  | Turn  Right |
> | S  | Reverse  |
> | SPACE  | Brake  |
> | MOUSE CURSOR | Look Around  |
